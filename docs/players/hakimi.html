Achraf Hakimi

Team: Morocco

Position: Right Back

Club: Paris Saint-Germain

Age: 23

Goals: 4

Assissts: 6

Preferred Foot: right

Why they matter: Hakimi has great experience already playing for some of the most famous clubs in the world including Borussia Dortmund, Real Madrid, and Inter Milan. His primary position is defensive minded but he also has the ability to race forward and put the ball in the back of the net. Hakimi is the undisputed leader of this squad even at a young age and will look to make an impact for his country in Qatar.
